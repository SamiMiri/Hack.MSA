Build a mobile-first "Adulting Simulator" web app - a choice-based scenario game where players navigate real-life adult situations and see the consequences of their decisions.

Core concept: Players face realistic adulting scenarios (signing a lease, handling an unexpected bill, choosing health insurance, etc.) and make choices that affect their life stats (money, stress, credit score, happiness). Think "Choose Your Own Adventure" meets real-life consequences. Like Mario Maker, but for adulting scenarios.

Key features to include:

Scenario Selection Screen
Browse available life scenarios to play through
Life Stats Bar
Always visible at top: Money, Stress, Credit Score, Happiness (or similar metrics)
Scenario Gameplay:

Present a realistic situation with context
Show 2-4 decision options
Player chooses one
Immediately show consequences and stat changes
Continue to next decision in the scenario (3-5 decisions per scenario)
End with outcome summary (success/struggle/failure)
Scenario Results Screen
Show final stats, what went well, what went wrong, lessons learned
Replay/Try Again
Let users replay scenarios with different choices
Progress Tracking
Track scenarios completed and best outcomes
Example Scenario: "First Month Living Alone"

Starting stats: $2000, Low Stress, No Credit Score, Happy
Decision 1: Rent is due but you're $200 short. Do you: (A) Ask parents for help (B) Skip groceries this week (C) Use credit card (D) Talk to landlord about payment plan
Each choice changes stats differently and affects next decisions
End result varies based on choices made
User flow:

User opens app → sees scenario menu → picks a scenario → reads situation → makes choice → sees immediate consequence → stats update → next decision appears → completes 3-5 decisions → sees final outcome and stats → can replay or try new scenario
Design style: Game-like, clean, mobile-first. Clear buttons for choices, animated stat changes, satisfying feedback. Should feel like playing a game, not taking a quiz.

Tech stack suggestions: React or Next.js, simple state management for stats, mobile-responsive, can start without backend (scenarios can be hardcoded JSON for MVP).

MVP scope: Build 1 complete scenario with 4-5 branching decisions, working stat system, outcome screen, and replay functionality. Make it feel polished and game-like. Add more scenarios after the core loop works.

Important: Focus on making choices feel meaningful - different decisions should lead to noticeably different outcomes and stat changes. The consequences should be realistic but not preachy.
